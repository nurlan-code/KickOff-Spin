Responsible Gaming

We care about healthy play habits. Here are tips, tools, and resources to help you play responsibly.

Healthy Play Guidelines

  • Play for fun — not to solve personal or financial issues.
  • Set time limits and take regular breaks.
  • Keep entertainment in balance with other life activities.
  • Disable notifications when you need a break.

Self-Management Tools

  • Daily reminders to encourage breaks.
  • Optional session time tracking in your profile.
  • Mute or reduce in-app promotional messages.

Signs You May Need Support

  • Play interferes with work, school, or relationships.
  • You feel guilt or anxiety related to play.
  • You struggle to stop despite negative consequences.

Help Resources

Consult local support organizations in your region. If urgent, contact professional mental health services.

Contact Us

We can point you to neutral resources. Use the form on the Contact page.
