Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

  • Profile data: nickname, avatar preferences.

2. How We Use Information

  • To operate and improve the site and gameplay experience.
  • To communicate updates when you opt-in (e.g., newsletter).
  • To protect against abuse, fraud, and misuse.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Limited data may be shared with service providers strictly to operate site features (e.g., email delivery), under confidentiality obligations.

4. Cookies

We may use minimal cookies for essential functions (e.g., session state). You can control cookies through your browser settings.

5. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your information. Contact us through the Contact page for requests.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect data from unauthorized access. No method is 100% secure; please use strong credentials and keep them private.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are for users 18+ only. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided data, contact us to remove it.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us via the Contact page.

We respect your privacy and use data responsibly to improve your experience.
